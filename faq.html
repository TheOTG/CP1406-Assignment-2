FAQ


How do I contact you? You can email us at frias@gmail.com, or call us at +65-44446677.

A product I've seen is no longer available. We have limited quantities of items and once an item sells out, we generally do not restock.

Do you have a different size? We only have the sizes listed on the product page in stock. We unfortunately only carry limited sizes in stock.

Do you make special orders? We unfortunately can not make special orders.

Do you restock items? We restock items once a month, early in the month.

Are your styling products for sale? Our styling products are not for sale.

Something is wrong with my order. Please contact us as soon as possible at frias@gmail.com. Once something has shipped, we can no longer make changes to your order.

What forms of payment do you accept? We accept all major credit cards, as well as Paypal.

How do I cancel an order? Write us as soon as possible at frias@gmail.com Once something has shipped, we can no longer cancel and refund an order.

How do I use my promo code? You can enter your promo code in your shopping cart, just above the checkout button.

Do I need to create an account to shop from you? You do not! You can check out as a guest during checkout.

Will I fit into a garment listed as one size/free size? Our one size garments are items that are meant to be worn oversized, are free flowing or adjustable. If you wear a size small to large, these items will generally fit you. If you are still unsatisfied, you may always use our free alterations services.
