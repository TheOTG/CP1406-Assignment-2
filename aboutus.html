FRIAS Kid's Wear

Once Upon a time... there was a boy who had a dream of saving the world. With his passion of Fashion he came up with an idea. On that day a new clothing brand was born(FRIAS), that could save the earth and spoke the language which children speak. All clothing was made of eco friendly cotton and clothing brand could capture the magical, passionate look and language of children.

FRIAS is a Kids Wear fashion outlet that aims to provide the highest quality, and most comfortable clothes for kids of all ages with our value-added services such as full body measurements to allow kids to have the best fit. Our core values are creativity, passion and to operate under a social and ecological protocol base on moderation, intelligence and cooperation.
